About

Hello! I'm Alic Szecsei. I am currently a Master's student in Computer Science at the University of Iowa, where I received my bachelor's degree in 2014. Computer science and interactive storytelling have long been my biggest passions.

As a game developer, I have a drive to create games that merge aesthetics with challenging, innovative gameplay. My favorite games include Shadow of the Colossus, Axiom Verge, Elite: Dangerous, and Mirror's Edge.
